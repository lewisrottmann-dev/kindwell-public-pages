Medical Disclaimer

Last updated: May 9, 2026

KindWell is a wellness logging app. It is not medical advice, medical care, a medical device, clinical monitoring, diagnosis, treatment, prevention, or emergency dispatch.

KindWell does not evaluate symptoms, verify medicine schedules, detect emergencies, contact caregivers automatically, or call emergency services automatically.

Talk with a licensed clinician before making decisions about symptoms, medicines, dosage, treatment, diet, activity, or any health condition.

If you think you may have a medical emergency, feel unsafe, or need urgent help, call local emergency services directly.

Caregiver summaries are informal notes created from information entered in the app. They are not medical records and should be checked before sharing or relying on them.
