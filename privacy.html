Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

KindWell stores wellness check-ins, medicine entries, family contact details, notes, and reminder preferences on your device.

This version does not require an account, does not use advertising, does not track you across apps or websites, and does not send your wellness information to a KindWell server.

If you share a caregiver summary, call a contact, or enable notifications, those actions use Apple system features on your device.

KindWell is not medical care, does not diagnose or treat health conditions, and does not provide emergency monitoring or dispatch.
